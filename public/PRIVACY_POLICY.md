# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## Introduction

Chandu UI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our open-source React component library and associated services.

## Open Source Nature

Chandu UI is an open-source project. This Privacy Policy applies to:
- Our website and documentation
- Our GitHub repository and related services
- Any services we provide related to Chandu UI

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **GitHub Contributions**: If you contribute to our repository, we collect information associated with your GitHub account (username, email if public, contributions)
- **Issue Reports**: Information you provide when reporting bugs or requesting features
- **Discussions**: Content you post in GitHub Discussions
- **Email Communications**: If you contact us via email (chanduui@outlook.in)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Analytics**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics through our website
- **GitHub Activity**: Public activity on our GitHub repository (commits, issues, pull requests)
- **Cookies and Tracking**: We may use cookies for website functionality and analytics

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal financial information
- Sensitive personal data beyond what you voluntarily share
- Information from third-party services without your consent

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Maintain and improve the Chandu UI library
- Respond to issues, bug reports, and feature requests
- Communicate with contributors and users
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our components
- Ensure security and prevent abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Public Information

As an open-source project:
- **GitHub Contributions**: All code contributions, issues, and pull requests are publicly visible
- **Discussions**: Public discussions are visible to all users
- **Documentation**: Usage examples and documentation are publicly available

### Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:
- **GitHub**: For hosting our repository and managing contributions
- **Analytics Services**: For understanding website usage (if applicable)
- **Hosting Providers**: For hosting our website and documentation

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:
- Secure hosting and infrastructure
- Regular security updates
- Access controls for sensitive data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

- **GitHub Data**: You can control your GitHub profile visibility and information
- **Email Communications**: You can opt-out of non-essential emails
- **Cookies**: You can disable cookies in your browser settings

### Data Deletion

- **Contributions**: Code contributions to open-source projects are generally permanent
- **Issues/Discussions**: You can delete or edit your own posts
- **Personal Information**: Contact us to request deletion of personal data we control

## Children's Privacy

Chandu UI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

Chandu UI is accessible globally. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.
